Help phlebotomists and earn every week.

Why join with us as Phlebotomist

Work on your schedule Work for a few hours in the mornings, or a day it's up to you. You are your own boss and you can choose when and how much you work.

Phlebotomist kit

We provide a phlebotomy kit for you which include all the accessories to do venipuncture. So you don’t have to worry about the things to complete your work.

Earn money

You'll make money by helping people. Being a pick2heal phlebotomist simply means that helping others and some extra cash on every week.

Requirements to join as phlebotomist

  • Be at least 21 years old
  • Academic qualification – BSc MLT / DMLT / BSc Nursing / Diploma in Nursing.
  • Minimum one year experience in venipuncture.

Documents

  • Valid driving license
  • Proof of residency in your city
  • Proof of all the academic qualification

Join process

  • Visit the nearest pick2heal centre in your city
  • Submit documents and photo
  • Attend verification process.
  • Collect Phlebotomy kit and start the work.
